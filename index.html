How to Use The Command Center

1. Find an RSS Feed: The easiest way is to search Google for "[Website Name] RSS Feed".

2. Add the Feed: Click the `+` button in the "My Feeds" panel. Paste the Feed Name and the full RSS URL.

3. View Your News: Simply click on any feed in your list to load the latest articles. Your feeds are saved in your browser.
